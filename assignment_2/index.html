This visualisation provide an overview of the World Happiness Report 2020.

World Happiness Map

The Happiness Ladder is used to rank people's satisfaction with respect to their present living conditions. The ladder is numbered from 0-10, where 10 represents the best whereas 0 presents the worst possible life and living conditions

The World Happiness Report is a survey of the state of global happinesss that ranks 156 countries by how happy their citizens perceive themselves to be.

*Countries with missing data are represented with grey areas.

Average Happiness Key Variables values by Region

The World Happiness Report uses six variables to explain the variation of happiness across countries. These variables are GDP per capita, social support, healthy life expectancy, freedom, generosity and absence of corruption.
